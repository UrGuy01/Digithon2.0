About Me

Hello, I'm Somaan Rauniyar, a passionate developer and student at SRM Institute of Science and Technology. I'm dedicated to creating innovative solutions that bridge the gap between technology and healthcare.

My Vision

MedMentors represents my vision of making healthcare information more accessible through artificial intelligence. By combining machine learning with medical knowledge, I aim to create a platform that helps people make informed decisions about their health.

The Project

MedMentors is an AI-powered healthcare assistant that provides preliminary medical recommendations based on symptoms. This academic project showcases the potential of AI in healthcare while maintaining a user-friendly approach.

Academic Background

As a student at SRM Institute of Science and Technology, I'm constantly learning and applying new technologies. This project represents the culmination of my academic knowledge and practical programming skills.

This project was developed as part of academic research and demonstrates the potential of AI in healthcare applications.
